About the creators

Play Well!

The time has come!!! The first annual ‘Play Well’ event is almost here. Join us to see your favorite master builders at work or find those rare sets at our marketplace. You might also want to test your skills in our build competition; lots of prizes to be won! Reserve your place now here.

‘Play Well’ is fun for the whole family. With over 30 vendors and over 20 master builders, there’s something for everyone. Find out more about the event by checking out our event calendar.

Our master builders travel from all over the world and have appeared at many events. Their creations are second to none and truly need to be seen to be believed. Whether you are an ‘AFOL’ (Adult Fan of Lego) or you’re just getting started, we’re sure that you will be inspired by these amazing builds.

This year's event will be held at Harbour Center 555 W Hastings St. Vancouver, BC from August 15 through August 17, 2022. Doors are from 10am to 5pm. Get your tickets now.

Creators

alex stanley

This young German builder has taken the Lego world by storm! Winner of the Golden brick, Platinum minifig, and BlockHead awards all before the age of 12. He is a true Master of the medium. This year we will be treated to his history of space travel diorama. His interactive approach are sure to delight Lego fans of any age.

allen blockly

Allen is 26 years old, from Toronto, Canada, and has been a master builder for over 15 years. His unique take on the ‘Star Wars’ universe has gained him global attention. His builds are a perfect mix of nostalgia and humor.

bill derman

Bill comes from London, England. His specialty is grand architectural pieces. His ancient Rome build is what put him on the map and this year he will be unveiling a future Mars colony build. His creativity and attention to detail is unrivaled and must be seen in person.

brix macfadden

This young and upcoming builder, from Dublin, Ireland, builds pieces inspired by surrealist Salvadore Dali. Her ‘melting clocks’ build has achieved worldwide attention and won her several awards. You’re not going to want to miss her appearance.

emma builder

Designer turned builder! Emma, from Mexico City, brings her unique approach to all her builds. Her whimsical fantasy builds will surely inspire any fan of dragons, wizards and the like. We are honored to welcome this talented young artist.

george masters

Planes, Trains, Automobiles….This young builder from Alberta, Canada is all about engineering. His working models of famous vehicles and structures are sure to impress. This is his first appearance but you’re going to want to keep an eye on this young dynamo!

legolas schmidt

Finnish master builder Legolas Schmidt, is a true tour de force! His creative, nature inspired, MOC (My Own creation) sets has made this young tycoon a cool $250,000 so far. This enterprising young artist must be seen.

susan bickheart

This 20 year old builder from Tennessee, winner of the golden brick award, has generously graced our event with her presence. Her builds are absolutely mind blowing; she brings a level of artistry to her craft that truly must be seen.

vanessa settler

Vanessa comes from Paris, France. Her creative and artistic renditions of historical events are both faithful and playful. This year she will be unveiling her ‘Lincoln Assassination’ build.

william hsu

The legend himself returns! Star of the hit show “build or bail” William will appear this year to show off his new build. He and his press agents are fairly tight lipped as to what we should expect to see but this young man never disappoints. You are not going to want to miss this once in a lifetime reveal!
